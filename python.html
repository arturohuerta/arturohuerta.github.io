Written Responce

When me and my partner made this we figured we wanted something halarious, and comedic. We used the first things that came to our minds like thanos, Sans Undertale and Mike Tyson. A few of our problems that were encountered while we made this was mostly with the looping. After a choise it just kept looping us back into the same question. We managed to fix this by reconecting our states, which connect to our second problem which was why, we kept looking trough and we could not figure it out until we saw that we had to put it in a global state. After that it was really smooth sailing being pretty easy. We first made all the endings giving them a name so later on we could be able to easily connect them. After we finished that we made everything connect, with the choises to every ending. Over all I found out this was a really fun project.

In our code our main features are the choices that the player has to make in order to get one of the main five endings. We started of with extremely simple things like Y for yes and N for no. However after a while we decided to have a different set of choices, these being attack or reason. There are multiple times there come into effect like when the player first meets Bernort, or John Wack. Another command we had was figuring out how to start over. Everytime a player gets to an ending they get brought back to the beginig shown in the video when the player gets the bad ending, or not epic ending. This lets the player jump right back into the game with nothing to bother them.
